**Increased Discovery Bound Resets.** The number of price-discovery bounds was increased for nearly all assets:

- **1 reset:** HOOD, INTC, PLTR, COIN, ORCL, AMD, MU, SNDK, CRCL, NFLX, SKHX, TSM, JPY, EUR, BABA, COPPER, NATGAS, SMSN, PLATINUM, CRWV, PALLADIUM, EWY, EWJ, LITE, MRVL, DRAM, CBRS, ARM, BB, QNT, AVGO, NBIS.
 
- **2 resets:** TSLA, META, MSFT, GOOGL, AMZN, MSTR, PURRDAT, NVDA, GOLD.
 

**Higher Leverage + Tighter Discovery Bounds.** For **TSLA, META, MSFT, GOOGL, AMZN**:

- Max leverage raised **10× → 20×**
 
- Discovery band tightened **0.10 → 0.05**
 

SPCX IPOP had it's discovery bound set to **0.30** from **0.20** and discovery bound resets to **3** from **7**.

Markdown versions of documentation pages are available by appending \`.md\` to page URLs; this page is available as \[Markdown\](https://docs.trade.xyz/changelog/funding-rate-formula-update.md). # Funding Rate Formula Update ### \*December 19, 2025 - Scaling Factor for Funding Rate Formula Update\* The default funding rate for crypto perps has remained unchanged at 0.01% / 8 hours since Bitmex first introduced the mechanism in 2016. Borrow rates for traditional asset classes (e.g., equities, commodities) are typically closer to SOFR + 1-2%. We reduced the funding rate across all XYZ markets by applying a scaling factor of 0.5 to Hyperliquid’s funding rate formula. This lowers baseline funding to \\~5.5% annualized and results in less aggressive funding during weekend price discovery as well.

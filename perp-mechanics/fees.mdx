trade[XYZ] uses a tiered fee structure based on your rolling 14-day trading volume. Fees are assessed daily (UTC) and a single tier applies uniformly across all perps, HIP-3 perps, and spot markets. Maker rebates are paid continuously per trade. The sections below explain standard fees, growth mode, and how to read the fee tier tables.

## Standard Fees

Standard fees for all HIP-3 assets are currently **2× the usual fees on validator-operated perp markets**, with 50% of those fees going to Hyperliquid and 50% to trade[XYZ]. You can read more about the HIP-3 fee framework in [Hyperliquid's documentation](https://hyperliquid.gitbook.io/hyperliquid-docs/hyperliquid-improvement-proposals-hips/hip-3-builder-deployed-perpetuals).

## Growth Mode

Growth mode may be enabled on certain assets. When active, all-in fees are reduced by **≥90%** from the standard fee rate. Both rebates and volume-contribution credits are also reduced by ≥90% under growth mode.

### Growth Mode Ineligibility

The following asset types are **not eligible** for growth mode:

- Crypto perps against any collateral
- Perps on crypto indexes, ETFs, or other baskets of crypto assets
- Perps on mathematical combinations including crypto assets (e.g., BTC + X)
- Perps on vehicles or wrappers that hold primarily crypto assets (e.g. MSTR)
- Perps tracking gold, because PAXG-USDC already tracks gold price (e.g. Gold)

## Fee Tiers

Fee tiers are based on your rolling 14-day weighted volume, assessed daily at UTC midnight. One tier applies across all perps, HIP-3 perps, and spot. Maker rebates are paid continuously per trade.

Hyperliquid staking discounts apply on top of the tiers below. You can read more about staking discounts in [Hyperliquid's documentation](https://hyperliquid.gitbook.io/hyperliquid-docs/trading/fees).

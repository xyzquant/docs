A perpetual derivative contract (or "perp") is a derivative product that allows you to speculate on an asset's price without an expiry date. Unlike traditional futures, which settle on a fixed date, you can hold a position indefinitely. Perps rely on a funding rate mechanism to keep the contract price aligned with the underlying spot price.

## What are XYZ perpetuals?

XYZ perpetuals are linear contracts, margined and settled in USDC. The oracle is denominated in USD, and no USDC/USD conversion is applied — economic exposure is therefore _quanto_, with USD profit and loss cash-settled in USDC.

## HyperCore vs. XYZ-bespoke components

XYZ perpetuals are listed on the XYZ HIP-3 deployment. Most of the core infrastructure is shared with Hyperliquid's native perp engine:

| Layer | Handled by |
|---|---|
| Order matching, order types | HyperCore |
| Funding calculations | HyperCore |
| Liquidations & auto-deleveraging | HyperCore |
| Oracle price | XYZ (bespoke) |
| Mark price | XYZ (bespoke) |
| External price | XYZ (bespoke) |

The three bespoke components — oracle price, mark price, and external price — are what allow XYZ markets to operate 24/7 across equities, indices, commodities, FX, and pre-IPO assets.

## The Relayer

The **Relayer** is the service responsible for computing the oracle, mark, and external prices for each XYZ asset and broadcasting those updates to HyperCore. trade[XYZ] manages a distributed set of Relayer instances that transmit updates approximately every **3 seconds**.

<Note>
  XYZ's oracle updater address is `0x1234567890545d1Df9EE64B35Fdd16966e08aCEC`. You can verify Relayer activity on-chain by monitoring transactions from this address.
</Note>

## The three bespoke price components

Each of the three XYZ-specific prices serves a distinct role in keeping markets safe and fairly priced:

| Component | Role | Detail |
|---|---|---|
| **Oracle Price** | The reference price used for funding and liquidations, derived from external market data | [Oracle Price →](/perp-mechanics/oracle-price) |
| **Mark Price** | A smoothed, manipulation-resistant price used to calculate unrealised P&L | [Mark Price →](/perp-mechanics/mark-price) |
| **External Price** | The real-time price sourced from external venues, used as the basis for the oracle | [External Price →](/perp-mechanics/external-price) |

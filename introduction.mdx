trade[XYZ] is a HIP-3 decentralized exchange (DEX) built on the Hyperliquid blockchain. It lists perpetual derivative contracts across equities, equity indices, commodities, foreign exchange, and pre-IPO assets — all margined and settled in USDC. Unlike traditional exchanges, trade[XYZ] markets operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, enabling price discovery over weekends, holidays, and extended sessions when underlying exchanges are closed.

## What is trade[XYZ]?

The XYZ protocol is a HIP-3 DEX that defines the market listings, oracle sources, leverage limits, and other parameters for XYZ perpetual markets. [HIP-3](https://hyperliquid.gitbook.io/hyperliquid-docs/hyperliquid-improvement-proposals-hips/hip-3-builder-deployed-perpetuals) allows independent builders to deploy and operate their own perpetual markets on Hyperliquid.

**trade[XYZ]** is the primary interface for accessing XYZ markets, though XYZ markets are also accessible via the Hyperliquid API and any compatible client. The protocol is non-custodial — trade[XYZ] never holds your funds or intermediates your trades.

## Key concepts

| Term | Description |
|---|---|
| **XYZ Protocol** | The HIP-3 deployment that governs market listings and parameters |
| **trade[XYZ]** | The trading interface for XYZ (and other Hyperliquid) markets |
| **Relayer** | The service that computes and broadcasts oracle, mark, and external prices every ~3 seconds |
| **HyperCore** | Hyperliquid's core matching engine — handles orders, funding, liquidations, and ADL |
| **IPOP** | Pre-IPO Perpetual — a cash-settled perp referencing an anticipated public listing |

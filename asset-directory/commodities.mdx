XYZ lists perpetual contracts on a range of commodities including precious metals, industrial metals, and energy. External pricing coverage runs **23/5**, from **Sunday 6:00 PM ET** to **Friday 5:00 PM ET**, with a daily gap from 5:00 PM to 6:00 PM ET during which internal pricing applies. This schedule reflects the underlying commodity markets' standard hours and the daily close/reopen cycle.

## External Price Derivation

The Relayer derives external prices differently depending on whether a liquid spot market exists for the commodity.

### Precious Metals

Liquid spot markets are available for **GOLD**, **SILVER**, **PLATINUM**, and **PALLADIUM**. The Relayer uses these spot markets directly as the external price source for each asset.

### Industrial Metals & Energy

Industrial metals and energy commodities do not have a single unified "spot" market in the way financial assets do. The underlying price of these assets varies by geography and contract specification.

To derive external prices for these assets, the Relayer uses an industry-standard approach: referencing a fixed set of **designated futures contracts**. The active contract is rolled from the **5th to the 10th business day** of the month according to a predefined weighting schedule.

Roll transitions occur during **internal pricing sessions**. Updated contract weightings take effect when the oracle switches back to external pricing at 6:00 PM ET.

#### Roll Weighting Schedule

| Business Day | Front Contract | Next Contract |
|---|---|---|
| 5th | 100% | 0% |
| 6th | 80% | 20% |
| 7th | 60% | 40% |
| 8th | 40% | 60% |
| 9th | 20% | 80% |
| 10th | 0% | 100% |

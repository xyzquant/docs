This page shows the active contract month for each futures-based commodity market on XYZ and the schedule for rolling from one contract to the next. Use it to understand when pricing transitions occur and how blended weightings apply during a roll window.

## How Rolls Work

The roll period runs from the 5th to the 10th business day of the month. During this window, the oracle price transitions from the front-month contract to the next-month contract via a daily blended weighting. Updated weightings take effect when the oracle switches back to external pricing at 6:00 PM ET each day.

<Note>
  All roll windows run from 5:30 PM ET on the start date to 5:30 PM ET on the end date.
</Note>

### Example Roll: WTIOIL Q6 → U6

The following example shows how WTIOIL transitions from the Q6 contract to the U6 contract over five steps:

| Date | Front-Month (Q6) | Next-Month (U6) |
|---|---|---|
| 2026-07-08 5:30 PM ET | 80% | 20% |
| 2026-07-09 5:30 PM ET | 60% | 40% |
| 2026-07-10 5:30 PM ET | 40% | 60% |
| 2026-07-13 5:30 PM ET | 20% | 80% |
| 2026-07-14 5:30 PM ET | 0% (roll complete) | 100% |
